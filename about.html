Afrin Jahan Moury

Architecture Graduate with a passion for design that bridges sustainability, human experience, and cultural context. Currently completing a Master of Architecture at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, specializing in Building Performance and Tall Building Design.

Her work spans a wide range of typologies and scales — from high-rise residential towers in Dhaka and a maritime cultural centre in Jeju, South Korea, to a thesis project transforming Dhaka's waste infrastructure into public parkland, recognized as a top-50 finalist in the Tamayouz International Award for Graduation Projects.

She brings both technical rigor and formal creativity to every project, with experience in BIM coordination, computational design, environmental simulation, and design-build practice.
