Ember Negative

Location Texas, USA
Site Area 44,000 sq. ft.
Type Fire Station
Faculty Erik Hemingway
Concept

Located within a growing urban community in Texas, Ember Negative reimagines the fire station as a symbol of protection, resilience, and public trust. Built with durable concrete block masonry, the project supports efficient emergency response while creating welcoming public spaces for education, gathering, and fire-safety awareness.

In a region increasingly shaped by extreme heat, wildfires, and rapid urban growth, the station acts as a visible anchor of security — transforming vulnerability into preparedness, empowerment, and collective safety.
