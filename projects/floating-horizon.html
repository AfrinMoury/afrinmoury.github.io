Floating Horizon

Location Clinton Lake, Illinois, USA
Site Area 620,000 sq. ft.
Type Rowing Centre — Group Project
Faculty Scott Murray
Contribution Building plans, diagrams & final renderings
Concept

The design envisions a floating horizon along the lakefront where an elevated steel ramp in a bright accent color extends over the landscape and water, offering the public a unique space to view rowing activities and competitions. The ramp becomes a visual highlight, contrasting with the surrounding trees and the calm surface of the lake.

Below and beside it, the boathouse and rowing center are built in engineered wood, expressing warmth, rhythm, and craftsmanship — grounding the boldness of the ramp in a material language that echoes the natural lakeside setting.
