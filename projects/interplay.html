Interplay

Location Bashundhara, Dhaka, Bangladesh
Site Area 1 acre
Type Apartment Building
Faculty Muzzafar
Concept

Our surroundings and our neighborhood affect us as well as they are also affected by us. The main idea was to create independence among the people and their surroundings and give them a breathable space.

The design explores the interplay between private dwelling and shared community — creating terraces, green corridors, and semi-public thresholds that blur the boundary between interior and exterior life. The building becomes not just a place to live, but a living ecosystem.
