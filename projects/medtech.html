Concept

This hospital design concept revolves around fostering a unique integration with nature, emphasizing a profound connection with green spaces. The module's design encapsulates inner green spaces, offering rejuvenating environmental refreshment.

This nurturing environment not only promotes healing but also aims to create enduring memories for individuals. Embracing nature within the hospital’s core fabric enhances the therapeutic journey for patients, staff, and visitors alike.
