Nautical

Location Jeju, South Korea
Site Area 43,560 sq. ft.
Type Maritime Centre — Group Project
Faculty Prof. Yun Yi
Contribution Horizontal mass development, diagrams & final renderings
Concept

Inspired by Jeju's black volcanic rocks and dynamic ocean waves, the architecture adopts fluid, layered geometries that echo natural and cultural harmony. Traditional Korean motifs — such as geometric patterns and symbolic artifacts — are abstracted into structural and decorative elements.

Interactive galleries are designed as immersive experiences, where traditional crafts meet digital storytelling, highlighting Korea's seafaring legacy. Organic forms created through computational design blend seamlessly with the dramatic coastal landscape of Jeju Island.
