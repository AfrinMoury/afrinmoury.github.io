Recycling Park

Location Aminbazar, Dhaka, Bangladesh
Site Area Existing 53 acres; Extension 80 acres
Type Waste Management Industry & Urban Park — Thesis
Faculty Ismat Hossain, Rishaad Mohammad Yusuff
Concept

Waste is often overlooked but has the potential to be a valuable resource. This thesis explores how waste can be transformed into a resource through human collaboration rather than isolation, reducing the physical and psychological distance between people and waste. By creating a transitional zone between urban recreation and industrial areas, the project aims to integrate social activities with the waste industry while giving space back to the community.

A green buffer around the site separates the public from industrial operations, and a secondary road improves accessibility. The design visually shields the industry, uses mechanized and futuristic buildings, and prioritizes recyclable materials. Ultimately, the project envisions a hybrid architecture where sustainability and social engagement coexist.
