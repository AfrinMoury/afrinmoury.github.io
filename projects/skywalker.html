Skywalker

Location Teigaon, Dhaka, Bangladesh
Site Area 87,900 sq. ft.
Type Skyscraper
Faculty Mujtaba Ahsan
Concept

The main idea was to create a dynamic form with a simple geometry which was eyecatching. The curves were created in a way that can cut off the wind speed, which reduces the overall cost of the building and make it more efficient. Also, the building gives people green connectivity which makes it more sustainable.
